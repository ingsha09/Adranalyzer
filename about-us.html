About The Project

The AdSense Readiness Analyzer is a passion project born from a desire to help fellow content creators, bloggers, and webmasters navigate the often-confusing technical requirements for Google AdSense. This tool was designed to provide a clear, automated, and instant analysis of a website's technical health, offering actionable insights to improve the chances of a successful AdSense application.

Meet the Creator

Ingsha Hang Subba

As a solo developer and web enthusiast, I, Ingsha Hang Subba, built this tool from the ground up. With a passion for creating useful and accessible web applications, I saw an opportunity to simplify a common challenge for many creators. My goal is to empower you with the technical knowledge needed to build better websites that meet industry standards.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide a free, reliable, and easy-to-use tool that demystifies the technical side of AdSense readiness. We believe that by providing clear feedback and recommendations, we can help creators focus on what they do best—creating high-quality content—while ensuring their site is technically sound.

Thank you for using Adranalyzer. Your support and feedback are invaluable as this project continues to evolve.
